Spell Fighting

Made using Unreal Engine 4

Platforms: Windows

11 people, 5 weeks

2023

Summary

Spell Fighting is a game where you want to knock your opponent off the platform. You can do this by casting various spells onto yourself and other players. Multiple spells of the same type can be picked up for a bigger/stronger version of that spell. You have 2 primary and 3 secondary spells. The shield and the grab spells are your primary spells, and are always available if you have enough mana. The 3 secondary spells have to be picked up, but can also be stacked.

Spells also interact with other spells, e.g. hitting a meteor with the wind spell causes the meteor to fly in the direction of the wind spell.
